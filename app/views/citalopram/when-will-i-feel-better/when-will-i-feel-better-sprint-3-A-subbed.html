Citalopram

When will I feel better?

You may not notice much improvement in your symptoms for a week or two until citalopram begins to take effect and it usually takes between four and six weeks before you will feel the full benefits.

Don't stop taking citalopram after a week or two just because you feel it isn't helping your symptoms. Give the medicine at least six weeks to work.
