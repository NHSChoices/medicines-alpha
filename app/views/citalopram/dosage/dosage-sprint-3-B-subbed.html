Citalopram

Citalopram: dosage information

Citalopram is available as tablets (10mg, 20mg and 40mg) and as oral drops (40mg/ml).

The usual dose of citalopram is 20mg daily in adults. However, citalopram may be started at a lower dose and can be increased to a maximum of 40mg daily (unless you are over 65 years or have liver problems when a maximum dose of 20mg daily is recommended).

Citalopram is not licensed for use in children under 12 years old but in 12-18 years olds the usual dose is 10mg daily increased to 40mg daily if necessary.

If you are taking the oral drops, four drops (8mg) is equivalent to taking ONE 10mg tablet.

Overdose warning!

If you take too much citalopram, contact your doctor immediately for advice. Overdose can be dangerous.
