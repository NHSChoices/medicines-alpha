Citalopram

Citalopram: dosage information

Tablets

Citalopram is available as 10mg, 20mg and 40mg strength tablets.

The usual dose is 20mg daily in adults. However, the starting dose of citalopram may be lower than 20mg daily and the dose can be increased to a maximum of 40mg daily (unless you are over 65 years of age or have liver problems, in which case youâ€™re advised a maximum dose of 20mg daily).

Citalopram is not licensed for use in children under 12 years old. In 12 to 18-years-olds the usual dose is 10mg daily that can be increased if necessary to a maximum of 40mg daily.

Oral drops

Citalopram is available as oral drops of 40mg/ml where four drops (8mg) is equivalent to a 10mg tablet.
