Citalopram

Dosage

Citalopram is available as 10mg, 20mg or 40mg tablets. It is also available as oral drops. Four drops is the equivalent of a 10mg tablet.

  • low dose = less than 20mg per day
  • medium dose = 20mg per day
  • high dose = 30-40mg per day

If you are over 65 years or have liver problems a maximum dose of 20mg daily is recommended.

Always follow the dosage you have been prescribed

Children

Citalopram is not suitable for children under 12.
A standard dose for 12-18 year olds is 10mg per day.

Overdose warning!

If you take too much citalopram, contact your doctor immediately for advice. Overdose can be dangerous.
