Citalopram

How long to take citalopram for

Once your condition is under control you're likely to continue taking citalopram for several more months to prevent symptoms returning.

You will need to discuss with your doctor the pros and cons of continuing to take citalopram as a longer-term treatment. The decision will depend on the type and severity of your initial symptoms, whether it's a one-off or recurring problem, how well citalopram worked for you, and whether the medicine has had any bothersome side effects.
