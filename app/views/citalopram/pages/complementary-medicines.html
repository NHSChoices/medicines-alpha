Taking complementary medicines with citalopram

Make sure that your doctor and pharmacist know you are taking citalopram before starting or stopping any complementary medicine.

Never take the herbal remedy St John's wort (Hypericum perforatum) at the same time as citalopram as this will increase your risk of side effects.
