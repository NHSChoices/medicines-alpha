Introduction

Pregabalin is used to treat epilepsy and anxiety.

It is also taken to treat nerve pain. This type of pain can be caused by different illnesses including diabetes and shingles or an injury.

Pregabalin is only available on prescription. It can be taken as capsules or a liquid that you drink.

The medicine is also called by various brand names, including Lyrica, Alzain, Lecaent and Rewisca.
