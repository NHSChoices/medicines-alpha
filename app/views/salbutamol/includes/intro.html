Introduction

Salbutamol is a medicine used to relieve symptoms of asthma and COPD such as coughing, wheezing and feeling breathless.

Salbutamol is available only on prescription.

It usually comes as an inhaler. It's sometimes given as tablets, capsules or syrup for people who can't use an inhaler very well.

Salbutamol inhalers can be called by brand names including Airomir, Asmalal, Easyhaler, Pulvinal, Salamol, Easi-Breathe, Salbulin and Ventolin.
