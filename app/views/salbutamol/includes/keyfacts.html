Key facts

  • Salbutamol usually comes in a blue inhaler.
  • Salbutamol inhalers are safe and effective with few side effects if used properly.
  • Using a salbutamol inhaler gives you on-the-spot fast relief from breathing problems.
  • Adults and children can have 1 or 2 puffs of a salbutamol inhaler up to a maximum of 4 times in 24 hours.
  • Salbutamol is safe to use in pregnancy and while breastfeeding.

Check the leaflet inside your medicine packet for information about the brand that you are taking.
