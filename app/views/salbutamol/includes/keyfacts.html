Key facts

  • Salbutamol inhalers are safe and effective with few side effects if you use them properly.
  • Salbutamol inhalers are called 'reliever' inhalers - they give you immediate relief from breathing problems.
  • Adults and children can have 1 or 2 puffs of a salbutamol inhaler when they need it up to a maximum of 4 times in 24 hours.
  • Salbutamol is safe to use in pregnancy and while breastfeeding.
  • Common brand names for salbutamol inhalers include Airomir, Asmalal, Easyhaler, Pulvinal, Salamol, Easi-Breathe, Salbulin and Ventolin.
