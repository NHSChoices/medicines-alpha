Taking salbutamol with other medicines

Some medicines can interfere with the way salbutamol works.

If you're taking other prescribed medicines that may not mix well with salbutamol your doctor will decide whether the benefits of taking both medicines outweighs the risks.

Mixing salbutamol with herbal remedies or supplements

There are no known problems with taking herbal remedies and supplements with salbutamol.

For safety, speak to your pharmacist before taking taking any herbal or alternative remedies.

Tell your doctor and pharmacist that you're taking any other medicines, including herbal remedies or supplements, before starting salbutamol.
