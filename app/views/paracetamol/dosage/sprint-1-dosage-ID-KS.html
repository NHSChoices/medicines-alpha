Paracetamol

Dosage

The total maximum dosage of paracetamol for an adult should not exceed EIGHT 500mg doses (4000 milligram of paracetamol) in any 24 hours.

This includes any paracetamol that is present in any other medicines you are taking.

The usual dose of paracetamol for an adult is to take ONE or TWO 500mg tablets or capsules as a single dose.

People with liver or kidney problems or who regularly drink more than the recommended amount of alcohol may be advised to take lower doses.

For children

Children between 6 and 12 years can be given a half to one tablet up to four times a day, never less than four hours apart.

Do not exceed four tablets in one day.

Paracetamol should NOT be given to children under 6 years old.
