Paracetamol

Who may not be able to take paracetamol

Ask your doctor or pharmacist about the safe dose of paracetamol if you:

  • are very thin
  • have a history of liver or kidney problems
  • are a heavy drinker
  • take medicines for epilepsy or tuberculosis
