Paracetamol

Interactions

What to avoid with paracetamol

Beware of paracetamol contained within other medicines.
You should not exceed the total recommended dose.

Warfarin

Avoid regularly use of paracetamol and discuss your options doctor if you are taking warfarin. Occasional use of paracetamol should not significantly affect its performance.

Epilepsy and tuberculosis medication

Talk to your doctor if you are taking medicines for epilepsy or tuberculosis, as this may mean that you should take lower doses of paracetamol.

St John's wort

You should check with your doctor or pharmacist if you are taking St John's wort as it may be necessary to reduce your paracetamol dose.

Alcohol

If you regularly drink more than the recommended amount of alcohol you should speak to your GP before taking paracetamol.
