Paracetamol

Side effects

Serious side effects

Stop taking paracetamol and seek medical help URGENTLY if you have any of these potentially serious side effects:

Allergic reactions:

Warning signs of a dangerous allergic reaction include:

  • rash; hives; itching
  • red, swollen, blistered, or peeling skin with or without fever
  • wheezing; tightness in the chest or throat; trouble breathing or talking; unusual hoarseness
  • swelling of the mouth, face, lips, tongue, or throat

Liver problems:

Warning signs of liver problems include:

  • dark urine
  • feeling tired
  • not hungry
  • upset stomach or stomach pain
  • light-coloured stools
  • vomiting
  • yellow skin or eyes

Kidney problems:

Warning signs include:

  • not being able to pass urine or a change in how much urine you pass

Skin reactions:

A very serious skin reaction (Stevens-Johnson syndrome/toxic epidermal necrolysis) may happen. Warning signs include:

  • red, swollen, blistered, or peeling skin (with or without fever)
  • red or irritated eyes
  • sores in your mouth, throat, nose, or eyes
