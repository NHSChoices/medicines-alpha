Paracetamol

Side effects

Stop taking the medicine and seek urgent medical help if you experience:

  • allergic reactions
  • liver problems
  • kidney problems
  • skin reactions

Call your doctor or get medical help if you have any of these side effects:

  • upset stomach or vomiting
  • bruising or bleeding
  • suspect an infection
