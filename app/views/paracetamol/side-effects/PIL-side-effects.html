Paracetamol

Side effects

Paracetamol 500g tablets

Possible side effects

Like all medicines, paracetamol can have side effects. Adverse effects of paracetamol are rare but hypersensitivity including skin rash can occur.

There are usually no ill effects due to paracetamol being used in the recommended dosage during pregnancy, but patients should follow the advice of their doctor regarding its use.

If you are concerned about these or if you notice any side effects not mentioned in this leaflet, please inform your doctor or pharmacist.
