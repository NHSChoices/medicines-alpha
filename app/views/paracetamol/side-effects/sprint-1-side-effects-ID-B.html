Paracetamol

Side effects

Common side effects:

  • upset stomach or vomiting
  • bruising or bleeding
  • an infection

Call your doctor or get medical help if you have any of these side effects.

Less common side effects:

  • allergic reactions
  • upset stomach or stomach pains
  • problems urinating
  • skin reactions

Stop taking the medicine and seek urgent medical help if you experience any of these.
