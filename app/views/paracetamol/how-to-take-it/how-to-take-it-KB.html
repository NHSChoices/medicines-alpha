Paracetamol

How to take it

  • Most people take paracetamol as needed for pain relief or to reduce fever up to a maximum of four times a day for as long as symptoms continue.
  • If you have constant pain, you can take paracetamol regularly up to four times a day, spreading out the doses as evenly as possible.
  • Paracetamol can be taken with or without food.
  • Paracetamol doses must be at least four hours apart.
  • When taking paracetamol check that you are not taking any other medicines (including over-the-counter medicines) that also contain paracetamol as you may end up taking too much and this can cause overdose and serious or fatal liver damage.
