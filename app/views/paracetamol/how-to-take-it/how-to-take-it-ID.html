Paracetamol

How to take it

Take paracetamol when needed for pain relief or to reduce fever for as long as the symptoms continue.

  • Take up to a maximum of four times a day
  • Repeat doses must be at least four hours apart
  • Paracetamol can be taken with or without food

Follow any specific instructions that are outlined on the packaging or leaflet with your medicine.
