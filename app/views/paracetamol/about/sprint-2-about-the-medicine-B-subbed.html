Paracetamol

About paracetamol

Paracetamol is a type of painkiller. It can also be used to reduce fever.

It's available as tablets, capsules, soluble tablets, oral liquid suspension and suppositories. Paracetamol is also available as an injection but this is usually only used in hospitals.

Paracetamol is sometimes combined with other painkillers such as codeine or dihydrocodeine or with an anti-sickness medicine. It's also an ingredient in many remedies bought over the counter to control pain, congestion and the symptoms of cold and flu.

Paracetamol may be known by one of its brand names such as Calpol, Disprol, Hedex, Medinol, and Panadol.
