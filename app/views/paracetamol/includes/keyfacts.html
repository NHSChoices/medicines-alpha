Key facts

  • Paracetamol takes up to an hour to work.
  • The usual dose of paracetamol is one or two 500mg tablets at a time.
  • Never take more than eight 500mg tablets in 24 hours.
  • Do not take paracetamol with other medicines containing paracetamol.
  • Paracetamol is generally safe to take in pregnancy and while breastfeeding at recommended doses.

Check the leaflet inside your medicine packet for information about the brand that you are taking.
