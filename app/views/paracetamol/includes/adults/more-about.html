More about paracetamol

How paracetamol works

Paracetamol seems to work by blocking chemical messengers in the brain that tell us we have pain.

When will I feel better?

Paracetamol takes up to an hour to work. It keeps on working for about 5 hours.

If the pain lasts for more than three days, see your doctor.

Taking paracetamol for a long time

It's safe to take paracetamol regularly for many years as long as you don't take more than the recommended dosage.

If paracetamol doesn't work

If paracetamol doesn't work, there are other types of painkiller that you can try including:

  • ibuprofen
  • codeine
  • aspirin

Ibuprofen and ibuprofen-like painkillers are sometimes available as creams or gels that you rub onto a specific part of the body that is painful.

Some painkillers are only available on prescription. Talk to your doctor or pharmacist about the other treatment options that might be more suitable for you.
