Who can take paracetamol

Paracetamol syrup and suppositories can be given to children over 2 months of age.

Paracetamol tablets should only be given to children aged 6 or older.

Paracetamol needs to be taken with extra care by some children.

Check with your doctor or pharmacist if your child:

  • is small for their age
  • has had liver or kidney problems
  • takes medicine for epilepsy
  • takes medicine for tuberculosis (TB)
