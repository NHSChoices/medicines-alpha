Giving paracetamol with other medicines

In general, paracetamol does not interfere with other medicines.

It's important, though, that your child does not take another medicine with paracetamol in at the same time.

Paracetamol isn't suitable for some children. Talk to your doctor if they take:

  • warfarin (paracetamol can increase the risk of bleeding)
  • medicine to treat epilepsy
  • medicine to treat tuberculosis (TB)

Tell your doctor or pharmacist if your child is taking another medicine, including herbal remedies and supplements, before starting paracetamol.
