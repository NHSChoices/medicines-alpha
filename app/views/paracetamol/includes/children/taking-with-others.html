Giving paracetamol with other medicines

Safe

The only safe painkiller to give to children alongside paracetamol is ibuprofen (Nurofen).

If your child is very distressed, you can alternate a dose of paracetamol with a dose of ibuprofen every 2-3 hours.

Avoid ibuprofen if your child has asthma, unless advised otherwise by your GP.

Unsafe

Do not give your child another medicine with paracetamol in at the same time as you risk giving them too much.

Paracetamol is an ingredient in lots of pain and common cold remedies including:

  • Lemsip
  • Night Nurse
  • Day and Night Nurse capsules

Never give aspirin to a child under the age of 16.
