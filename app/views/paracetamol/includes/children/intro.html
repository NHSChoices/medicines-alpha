Introduction

Paracetamol is a common painkiller available without a prescription from shops and pharmacies. It can also be used to reduce fever (a temperature above 38C).

For older children, paracetamol is available as tablets. It may be called by brand names such as Disprol, Hedex, Medinol and Panadol.

For young children, paracetamol comes as a syrup (called Calpol). It is also available on prescription as suppositories (capsules inserted into the back passage).
