Paracetamol: other treatment options

Paracetamol is safe and effective but there are several other types of painkiller that you may be able to use instead of it including:

  • non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), such as ibuprofen
  • opioid analgesics, such as codeine

Some NSAIDs have the advantage of being available as creams or gels which may be a good choice if you have aches and pains in a specific part of your body, for instance joint pain or back or muscle strain.

As some painkillers that are an alternative to paracetamol are only available by prescription, it may be worth talking to your doctor or pharmacist about the best choices for you.
