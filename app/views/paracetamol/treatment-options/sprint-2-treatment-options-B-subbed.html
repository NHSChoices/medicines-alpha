Paracetamol

Paracetamol: other treatment options

Other types of painkiller that can be used instead of paracetamol include:

  • non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, or NSAIDs (such as ibuprofen). Some NSAIDs are also available as creams or gels.
  • opioid analgesics (such as codeine)

Some of these painkiller options are only available by prescription. Talk to your doctor or pharmacist about the choices that might be most appropriate for you.
