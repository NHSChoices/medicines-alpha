Paracetamol

Paracetamol: pregnancy and breastfeeding

There is no evidence that paracetamol tablets will harm your baby if you are pregnant.

Paracetamol is excreted in breast milk but in insignificant amounts.

However, if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, if you think you may be pregnant or if you are trying for a baby, you should let your doctor know.

Ask your doctor or pharmacist for advice before taking any medicine.
