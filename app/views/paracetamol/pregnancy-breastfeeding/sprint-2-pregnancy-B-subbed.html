Paracetamol

Taking paracetamol in pregnancy and when breastfeeding

Paracetamol is safe to take in pregnancy and during breastfeeding.

Paracetamol is the first choice of painkiller during pregnancy as studies have shown it has no ill effects.

Paracetamol passes into breast milk but not in large enough amounts to affect the baby.
