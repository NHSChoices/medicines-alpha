How to cope with side effects

What to do about:

  • feeling sick or vomiting - take metformin with food to reduce the chances of feeling sick, vomiting or having diarrhoea or stomach ache. It may also help to start taking metformin at a low dose and slowly increasing it over several weeks
  • a metallic taste in the mouth - if you find that metformin is giving you a metallic taste in the mouth, try chewing sugar-free gum

You can report any suspected side effect to a UK safety scheme.
