Metformin is a medicine used to treat type 2 diabetes and sometimes polycystic ovary syndrome.

Type 2 diabetes is a condition where the body doesn’t make enough insulin, or the insulin that it makes doesn't work properly. This can cause high blood sugar levels (hyperglycemia).

Meformin works by lowering your blood sugar levels. It’s available on prescription as tablets and as a liquid that you drink.
