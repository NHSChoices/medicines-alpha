Pregnancy and breastfeeding

Tell your doctor if you plan to get pregnant or become pregnant while taking this medicine.

It's not clear if metformin causes an increased risk to an unborn baby. Your doctor may be happy for you to keep taking metformin, either alone or in combination with insulin.

You can take metformin while you're breastfeeding. Metformin passes into breast milk but the amount is too small to affect your baby.
