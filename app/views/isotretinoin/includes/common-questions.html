Common questions

Can I drink alcohol with isotretinoin?

Yes, you can drink alcohol with isotretinoin capsules or gel.

When will it start to work?

This can vary. Your acne may get worse when you first take isotretinoin capsules, but will then normally improve after seven to 10 days and from then onwards.

For isotretinoin gel, it may take six to eight weeks before your acne starts to improve. If your acne has not improved after eight weeks or gets worse, make an appointment to see your GP.

How long will I take it for?

A course of isotretinoin capsules usually lasts for 16-24 weeks (around four to six months). Acne will normally keep improving for up to eight weeks after stopping. After that time most people do not need to carry on taking isotretinoin capsules although some people will need more courses.

For isotretinoin gel, you will probably need to use the gel for longer than six months to stop the acne from coming back.

Can I drive with isotretinoin?

Isotretinoin gel should not affect your ability to drive.

Isotretinoin capsules can make you feel sleepy and dizzy. You may also not see as well at night. If this happens to you, don’t drive or use machinery or tools until you have stopped treatment.

Will it affect my fertility?

Isotretinoin capsules or gel do not reduce a man's or woman’s fertility.
