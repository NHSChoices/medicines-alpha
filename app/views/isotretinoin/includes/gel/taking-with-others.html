Taking with other medicines

Some medicines and isotretinoin gel can interfere with each other and make it more likely that you will have side effects.

Tell your doctor or pharmacist if you’re taking any other medicines, including herbal remedies and supplements, before starting treatment with isotretinoin gel.

Tell your doctor if you’re taking these medicines before you start isotretinoin gel:

  • other acne medicines as using both together may make skin irritation worse
  • benzoyl peroxide cream or gel (another acne treatment) as it may make isotretinoin gel less effective.

Mixing isotretinoin with complementary remedies

There is very little information about taking herbal and complementary remedies while you use isotretinoin gel.

For safety, speak to your pharmacist before taking isotretinoin gel if you routinely take any herbal or alternative remedies.
