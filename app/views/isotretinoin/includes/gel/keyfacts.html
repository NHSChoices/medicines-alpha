Key facts

  • Isotretinoin gel is usually applied once or twice a day.
  • It may take a couple of months before you see it working.
  • The most common side effects of isotretinoin gel are red, sore or dry sensitive skin and skin being sensitive to sunlight. These side effects go away when you stop the treatment.
  • Isotretinoin gel can make your skin sensitive to sunlight. For safety, stay out of bright sun and use a high factor sun cream even on cloudy days.
