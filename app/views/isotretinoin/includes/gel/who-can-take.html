Who can take isotretinoin gel

Isotretinoin gel is for teenagers and adults with mild to moderate acne.

Do not use isotretinoin gel in children under the age of 12 or before puberty.

Isotretinoin gel may not be suitable for some people.

Tell your doctor before starting treatment with isotretinoin gel if you:

  • get eczema
  • have had skin cancer
  • you are pregnant or think you could be or you are breastfeeding

Do not use isotretinoin gel if you are pregnant or think you might be pregnant or you are breastfeeding.
