Important advice for women about isotretinoin gel

Don’t use isotretinoin gel if you are pregnant or if you think you are pregnant. This medicine can harm a baby.

  • Use reliable contraception to prevent pregnancy while you are using isotretinoin gel.
  • If you become pregnant during treatment with isotretinoin gel, stop using it and tell to your doctor.
  • Don't breastfeed while you are using isotretinoin gel. This medicine can get into breast milk and harm your baby.
