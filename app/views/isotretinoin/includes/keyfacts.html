Key facts

  • Isotretinoin capsules are usually taken once or twice a day straight after a meal or snack. Isotretinoin gel is usually applied once or twice a day.
  • Acne normally improves after taking isotretinoin capsules for a week to 10 days. For isotretinoin gel, it may take a couple of months before you see it working.
  • Most people have clear skin after a four to five month course of isotretinoin capsules. For isotretinoin gel, you will probably need to use the gel for longer to stop the acne from coming back.
  • The most common side effects of isotretinoin capsules are headaches, aching muscles and joints, dry sensitive skin and skin being sensitive to sunlight. For isotretinoin gel, the most common side effects are red, sore or dry sensitive skin and skin being sensitive to sunlight.
