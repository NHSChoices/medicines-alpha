Can I drink alcohol?

Yes, you can drink alcohol with isotretinoin capsules.

How long does it take to work?

Your acne may get worse when you first start treatment with isotretinoin capsules, but will then normally start to improve after 7 to 10 days.

How long will treatment last?

A treatment course of isotretinoin capsules usually lasts for 16 to 24 weeks (around 4 to 6 months). Acne will normally keep improving for up to 8 weeks after stopping treatment. After that time most people have permanently clear skin, but a few will need another round of treatment.

Can I drive or ride a bike?

Isotretinoin capsules can make you feel sleepy and dizzy. You may also not see as well at night. If this happens to you, don’t drive, cycle or use machinery or tools until you have stopped treatment.

Will it affect my fertility?

Isotretinoin capsules do not reduce a man's or woman’s fertility.

For women, it is safe to try for a baby from one month after treatment with isotretinoin capsules has finished.
