Like all medicines, isotretinoin capsules can cause side effects although not everyone gets them. Side effects will usually go away when you stop treatment.

Common side effects

The common side effects of isotretinoin capsules happen in around one in 10 people. They include:

  • dry skin and lips – for safety, don’t have any waxing, dermabrasion or laser skin treatment while you are taking this medicine and for at least 6 months after you stop as this could cause scarring or skin irritation
  • skin becoming more sensitive to sunlight – for safety, stay out of bright sun and use a high factor sun cream (SPF 15 or above) even on cloudy days. Do not use a sun-lamp or sun-beds
  • dry eyes
  • dry mouth
  • dry nose and nosebleeds
  • headaches and general aches and pains

It is very rare, but isotretinoin capsules can sometimes cause or worsen depression and even make people feel suicidal.

Tell your doctor straight away if you become depressed or think about ending your life while taking isotretinoin capsules.

Serious side effects

Serious side effects are rare and happen in less than one in 1,000 people

Stop taking isotretinoin capsules and tell a doctor straight away if you get:

  • depression or mood changes - symptoms can include anxiety, aggression and violence, changes in mood or suicidal thoughts
  • stomach problems – severe pain in your tummy, with or without bloody diarrhoea, feeling sick and being sick can be signs of a serious problem called pancreatitis
  • a serious skin rash that peels or has blisters. The skin rash may come with eye infections, ulcers, a fever and headaches
  • muscle weakness – including difficulty moving your arms or legs and painful, swollen or bruised areas of the body. Dark pee can also be a sign of muscle weakness
  • yellow skin or eyes, difficulty peeing or feeling very tired. These are all signs of liver or kidney problems
  • a bad headache that does not go away and makes you feel sick or vomit
  • sudden changes in eyesight including not seeing as well at night

Serious allergic reaction

The warning signs of a serious allergic reaction are:

  • skin rash
  • wheezing
  • tightness in the chest or throat
  • having trouble breathing or talking
  • unusual hoarseness
  • swelling of the mouth, face, lips, tongue, or throat

These are not all the side effects of isotretinoin capsules. For a full list see the leaflet inside your medicine packet.
