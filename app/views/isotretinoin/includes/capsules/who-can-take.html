Isotretinoin capsules are for teenagers and adults with severe acne.

Isotretinoin capsules contain soya. Do not take this medicine if you have an allergy to soya.

Isotretinoin capsules contain sorbitol. Do not take this medicine if you have an inherited digestive disorder called fructose intolerance.

Do not give isotretinoin capsules to children under the age of 12 or before puberty.

Do not take isotretinoin capsules if you are pregnant or think you might be pregnant or you are breastfeeding.

  • are pregnant or think you could be or you are breastfeeding
  • have ever had an allergic reaction to isotretinoin or any other medicine
