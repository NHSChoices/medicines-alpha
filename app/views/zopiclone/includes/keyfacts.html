Key facts

  • Zopiclone takes about an hour to work.
  • Zopiclone is usually prescribed for just 2 to 4 weeks. This is because your body gets used to it quickly, and after this time it is unlikely to have the same effect. Your body can also become dependent on it.
  • Common side effects are a metallic taste in your mouth, a dry mouth and daytime sleepiness.
  • Do not drink alcohol while you are on zopiclone. Having the two together can make you go into a deep sleep where you find it difficult to wake up.
  • Zopiclone is also called by the brand name Zimovane.
