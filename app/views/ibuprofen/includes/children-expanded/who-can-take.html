Who can take ibuprofen

Ibuprofen:

  • syrup can be given to children over 3 months of age
  • tablets and capsules can be given to children aged 6 or older
  • granules can given to children aged 12 or older

However, ibuprofen needs to be given with extra care to some children.

Check with your doctor or pharmacist if your child:

  • is allergic to other drugs for inflammation or has asthma
  • has chicken pox, as taking ibuprofen can cause server skin reactions
  • has asthma
  • has liver or kidney problems
