Giving ibuprofen with other painkillers

Safe

The only safe painkiller to give to children alongside ibuprofen is paracetamol.

If your child is very distressed, you can alternate a dose of ibuprofen with a dose of paracetamol every 2 to 3 hours.

Unsafe

Ibuprofen is an ingredient in lots medicines that you can buy from the supermarket or pharmacy.

Do not give your child another medicine with ibuprofen in at the same time as you risk giving them too much.

Never give aspirin to a child under the age of 16.
