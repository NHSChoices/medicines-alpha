Key facts

  • Ibuprofen takes 20 to 30 minutes to work if you take it by mouth. It takes a day or two to work if you apply it to your skin.
  • Ibuprofen is an anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). It works by reducing hormones that cause pain and swelling in the body.
  • Paracetamol is usually the best treatment for most types of pain but ibuprofen is better for period pain or toothache. Some people find ibuprofen better than paracetamol for back pain.
  • If you have muscle or joint pain in a particular part of your body, such as a strain, sprain or a sports injury, applying ibuprofen directly to your skin is a good option to try before tablets. You are less likely to get side effects if you use ibuprofen in this way.

Common brand names

Nurofen, Brufen, Calprofen, Fenbid, Ibugel, Ibuleve
