Key facts

  • If you take gliclazide once a day, it’s best to take it in the morning before breakfast.
  • If you take it more than once a day, take it before a meal so your blood sugar level doesn’t go too low.
  • Gliclazide can sometimes make your blood sugar level too low (hypoglycaemia). Carry some sweets or fruit juice with you to help when this happens.
  • Gliclazide may make you put on weight.
  • Do not drink alcohol on an empty stomach while taking gliclazide - it may make you blood sugar level drop too low.
Info:

Check the leaflet inside your medicine packet for specific information about the brand that you are taking.
