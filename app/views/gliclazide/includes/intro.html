Gliclazide is a medicine used to treat type 2 diabetes.

Type 2 diabetes is a condition where the body doesn’t make enough insulin, or the insulin that is made can't work properly. This causes high blood sugar levels (hyperglycemia).

Gliclazide works by lowering your blood sugar. It’s available as tablets on prescription.
