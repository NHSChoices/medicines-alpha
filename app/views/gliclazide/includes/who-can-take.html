Who can take gliclazide

Gliclazide isn't suitable for some people. Tell your doctor before starting the medicine if you:

  • have had an allergic reaction to gliclazide or its ingredients, to other medicines from the same group (sulfonylureas), or to other similar medicines (sulfonamides)
  • have insulin-dependent diabetes (type 1)
  • have ketone bodies and sugar in your urine
  • have severe kidney or liver disease
  • have a health problem called porphyria
  • are taking miconazole (a treatment for fungal infections)
  • are breastfeeding
  • have an illness called G6PD-deficiency
  • need to have surgery

Gliclazide is not recommended for children.
